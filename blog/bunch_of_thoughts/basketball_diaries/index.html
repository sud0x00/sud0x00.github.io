My Thoughts on The Basketball Diaries

                                                                                29th Nov , 2022

I don’t know the exact reason I watched this today. I was looking for some random movie under 2 hours so that I can get some good sleep after I finish watching it (it’s 11:26 now).

I had downloaded this 2 days ago along with some Jake Gyllenhaal movies.

The movie starts with Catholic vibes which made me think that this might be boring. But then you get to the point where Jim is getting spanked by The Father with a cricket bat. The four friends cut classes and run off to sniff something similar to nail polish and have some fun. Then comes the good part , Jim narrates that their team is the best catholic team while one of his friends (Pedro) goes on to steal stuff from the enemy team. Events like this show that the friends are people whom you aren’t supposed to mess around with.

Later in the movie Jimmy goes to visit his friend , Bobby who’s suffering from leukemia , which shows that he cares a lot for the best player on their catholic team.

This is where Jimmy’s downfall arc starts. He loses his vcard , gets into drugs & loses Bobby.

Jimmy is introduced to heroin by Pedro which helps him write better poems in the beginning. But things got way out of hand when he got expelled from his school and he quit his basketball team.

After some unexpected turn of events his old friend Reggie who was a junkie at one point tries to help him. It doesn’t work out so well for Jimmy but after doing 6 months in a prison he’s a clean guy.

It was one helluva movie. There wasn’t a single moment where I felt bored. The cast was amazing too.

The movie had good 60’s NY vibes which is something I like a lot.

Leonardo DiCaprio played an amazing role. There’s not much I can say about him.

Jim Caroll’s Cameo was pretty interesting.

Of course the same is the case with Mark’s acting which was lit.

Lorraine Bracco who’s the jew wife in Goodfellas and the psychiatrist in Sopranos played a great part of NY mom.

But Ma ! You could’ve educated your son the bad effects of drugs.

Michael Imperioli , another dude who’s acted in Sopranos & Goodfellas played a good short (best player who’s 1.73m kek) role in this movie.
